This is a collection of pages and tools for the Folding@home project.

Pages and tools:

  • Contribute - Shows some of the many ways you can contribute to Folding@home.
  • Project Progress - Shows the progress of projects.
  • Participating Organisations - An incomplete listing of organisations and entities that've announced their participation in Folding@home for COVID-19 research.
  • Project Base Credit Calculator - Determine the appropriate Base Credit for a project to meet your target PPD.
  • WU Credit Checker - Checks to see if a WU has been completed and credit granted.
