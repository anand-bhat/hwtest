This page lets you check if your Folding@home Work Unit (FAH WU) has been credited. Show instructions:

Copy the line from the FAH log that has the Project Id, Run, Clone and Gen (PRCG) numbers, paste that into the form and click "Check". Alternatively, enter the Project Id, Run, Clone and Gen (PRCG) numbers and click "Check".

Note: The line from the log should look like this: 
12:59:59:WU01:FS01:Sending unit results: id:01 state:SEND error:NO_ERROR project:12345 run:0 clone:1 gen:2 core:0x22 unit:0x000000000000000000012345
Click the "Check" button.
